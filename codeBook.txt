The Code Book of the dataset in out.txt

This dataset is created from:
https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip

Each of the variables of the dataset represent an average mean or standard deviation of Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones  from 30 people, by tracking accelerometer and gyroscope 3-axial raw signals tAcc-XYZ and tGyro-XYZ. 
These variabes include 53 averages for means of different measures and 33 averages for measure for standard deviation with respect to the 30 people participated in the study. The activities they performed and the respective codes for the variable "activity" are:
1 WALKING
2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
4 SITTING
5 STANDING
6 LAYING

The people participated are coded using an index 1:30. This variable is coded under "Subjects"
